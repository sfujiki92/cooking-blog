What you'll need:

  • Canola Oil
  • Longonisa Sausage
  • Eggs
  • Tomatoes
  • White Rice
  • Apple Cider Vinegar

Directions

  1. Heat the pan
  2. Cook sausage thoroghly
  3. Once cooked, use leftover oil to fry white rice
  4. Cook the eggs (sunny-side up is the traditional way of preparing this)
  5. In a small bowl, add diced tomatoes with salt to taste
  6. Serve with apple cider vinegar
